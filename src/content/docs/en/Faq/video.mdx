### FLV

Low overhead, continuous writing, not resistant to power outages, limited progress bar dragging in browser online preview.

### MKV

Resistant to power outages, easy to repair, some browsers do not support online audio decoding.

### TS

Resistant to power outages, easy to repair, limited progress bar dragging in browser online preview.

### MP4

Not resistant to power outages, suitable for archiving and distribution.
